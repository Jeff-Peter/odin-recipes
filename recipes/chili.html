Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 tbsp Chili Powder
  • 2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 tsp Cumin Powder
  • 1 Large White Onion
  • 2 tsp Black Pepper
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 3 Cloves Garlic
  • 1 (32 oz) Can Crushed or Diced Tomoatoes
  • 3/4 Cup Red Wine Vinegar

Directions

  1. Dice onions
  2. Dice garlic cloves
  3. Add ground beef to pan and begin cooking on medium
  4. Add cayenne, chili powder, cumin, salt and pepper to ground beef
  5. Add onions and garlic to ground beef after 5 minutes
  6. Cook ground beef until browned
  7. Add red wine vinegar and can of tomoatoes to ground beef
  8. Turn heat to low and let simmer 45 minutes
