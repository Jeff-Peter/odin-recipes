Ingredients

  • 1 lb Sirloin Steak
  • 1 large Onion
  • 2 Bell Peppers (any color)
  • 8 oz Butter
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 1 Pack Hoggie Subs
  • 1 Pack Pepper Jack Cheese Slices
  • Mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Dice onions
  2. Dice Bell Peppers
  3. Cut sirloin into thin slices
  4. Add butter to cast iron skillet and heat on low until melted
  5. Add sirloin to cast iron skillet
  6. Once steak is partially cooked add peppers and onions to skillet
  7. Cook steak, peppers and onions in skillet until done
  8. Turn heat to low and cover steak, onions and peppers in pepper cheese
  9. Toast hoggie subs
  10. Add mayonnaise to hoggie
  11. Transfer cooked ingredients into hoggie subs
