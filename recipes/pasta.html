Ingredients

  • 1 lb Ground Pork
  • 1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 tsp Garlic Power
  • 1 Jar Pasta Sauce
  • 1 Box Pasta Noodles
  • Salsa (Optional)
  • Cheese of your choice (Optional)

Directions

  1. Boil pasta according to box instructions
  2. Add ground pork and seasonings to skill and cook until browned
  3. Mix pasta, salsa and pork to large pot and mix
  4. Optionally add salsa
  5. Serve with cheese
